Greetings! DoDares is a "fun" & "interactive" game where you and your partner take turns to face random dares.

Select the type below, Harcore isn't for the weak.

Game Instructions

The game is simple:

  • Enter your name and your partner's name.
  • Press "Play" to start the timer and reveal your dare.
  • Complete the dare before time runs out!
  • You can shuffle for a new dare or skip as needed.

Warning: This game is only for Adults 18+.
If you are under age, please do not proceed and close this window.
